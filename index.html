Earthquakes in Oklahoma

I'm not a geologist or seismologist, but I have been reading a lot about Earthquakes in Oklahoma. I'm building this visualization to help people see what's going on.

Below in red are injection wells, in yellow are earthquakes sized by magnitude, and the lines in a light color are the state's fault lines.

The map will be more and more interactive as I continue to build it. I'll add filtering options, charts, and a timeline for futher analysis. Click on a counties to zoom the map. Please tell me what you think below.
